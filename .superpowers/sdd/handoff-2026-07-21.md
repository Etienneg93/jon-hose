## SPEC STATUS — the fight as built vs the written spec

`docs/superpowers/specs/2026-07-20-ass-man-fight-design.md` describes the
fight AS FIRST BUILT. Eight user-directed playtest rounds have superseded
much of it. **This handoff is the source of truth for as-built; the spec
doc has NOT been rewritten** (candidate task if the user wants the doc
current before release). What changed vs spec:

- **P1**: Cheek Clap cone → **Big Clap**: 1.6s charge that SUCKS Jon in
  (x-pull 85, dash breaks), held clap frame, then an expanding blast front
  to the drawn rx-85 boundary (moving rim = hit). Hip Check gained a
  sonic-boom launch + a wind-WAKE gust lane along the dash. Toilet Toss is
  common artillery (cd 7s): every OTHER throw the porcelain stands up as a
  Bidet Turret (`_bossTurret`, cap 2). Mix ≈ 40/40/20 clap/hip/toss.
  Movement: he never walks — upright HOVER drift (new pose).
- **P2**: waypoint flight patrol (flySpeed 190, depth drift, horizontal
  SOAR pose) at airZ 72. Ranged alternates on airfire cadence: a 3-bolt
  volley (airclap frame, marked mini ellipses) ↔ a **chasing beam**
  (megaman charge FX at the hand → 2.2s beam whose ground spot starts
  under HIM and chases at 95 px/s — outrunnable; rim = hit, ticks via
  takeHit; beam also holds the airclap frame). Slam: windup WHERE HE IS,
  telegraph chases Jon through the pause, locks at dive start, DIAGONAL
  dive onto the lock; landing = ass-contact slam frame → exhaust recovery
  read + a pressure RING (expanding rim, dmg+shove). No more clap-back
  lane waves, no teleport.
- **P3**: storm entry is a ceremony — fly UP (riseup), soar to arena
  center, DIVE-LAND (slam frame, thump), megaman charge beat, then 4 rings
  @125 px/s with a 46° gap rotating 55°/ring. **IMMUNE during the entry
  flight + circles, with a TORNADO wrap as the tell** (contact damage
  live). Openings: 2.6s exhaustion at PLAIN damage — the 1.25× multiplier
  was REMOVED (root cause of him melting) — and the 6s brawl window
  (P1 kit at 1.4s cadence) between storms, then re-ceremony.
- **Transitions**: P1→P2 rises through the beat (riseup); P2→P3 DESCENDS
  through the beat from wherever the gate caught him (slam pose, thump) —
  never snaps. Gates wait for in-flight moves; sequential-clamped.
- **Ambient wind**: 1/2/3 boss gust lanes by phase in non-overlapping
  depth THIRDS, random slot activation, 8–14s lifespans (reshuffling),
  pushMult 1.35, die at the kneel.
- **Facing hysteresis** (10px deadband). **Storm/beam/ring/bolt fx** all
  dark-underlay + bright-core (readable on clouds).

Unchanged from spec: hp 2200, gates 0.66/0.33 on hp fraction, 1.6s invuln
